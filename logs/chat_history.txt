Q: eta

- **ETA Expiry Date**: The last date allowing entry into India (per Source 2, Page 77).  
- **Visa Expiry Date**: The last date the visa remains valid (per Source 2, Page 77).  

If arrival at the immigration counter occurs **after the ETA expiry date** due to flight delays, entry may not be permitted unless a **transit visa** is obtained for extended waiting periods (per Source 5, Page 78).  

For transit purposes, a 24-hour stay in the airport is allowed for connecting flights, but longer stays require a transit visa.
